NEED HELP?

Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about
our services, therapy, and mental well-
being.

How do I book a therapy session?
+
You can book a therapy session through our website by selecting your preferred therapist and time slot.
Are online sessions available?
+
Yes, we offer secure and confidential online sessions via video calls.
What’s the difference between therapy and coaching?
+
Therapy focuses on mental health and emotional healing, while coaching supports personal and professional growth.
Do I need a subscription to access services?
+
No, you can access services on a per-session basis or choose a subscription for discounted rates.
Can I switch therapists if I don’t feel the right connection?
+
Absolutely. We encourage you to find the therapist who’s the best fit for you.
Is my information and session history kept confidential?
+
Yes, all your data is encrypted and stored securely in compliance with privacy regulations.
